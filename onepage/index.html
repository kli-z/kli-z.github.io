What is this site?

Security vulnerabilities are shockingly commonplace. As such, it is becoming increasingly important that users of all types be educated on when exploits are found so that account credentials can be changed (or whatever other sort of action needs to be taken).

This site aims to assist users such as yourself in taking the proper actions to keep you informed on recent security vulnerabilities. All we ask is an email address. With it, default behavior in handling it will be to send you emails when popular enough sites or services are attacked; however, we also allow you to receive daily emails in a newsletter fashion, or to list sites for which you would like to be notified. For more information on how your email address will be handled, please visit our privacy policy statement. Alternatively, you may proceed directly to signup.

Privacy Policy

We will never share your information about you (your email or your preferred sites, if you choose to list them) to other companies. Analytics this site does collect are solely for internal use.

If, at any time, you wish to opt out of these notifications, you may email us with that address at feedback@securitynews.com requesting to be removed from our mailing list. However, we reserve the right to continue to use analytics we have collected through you to better improve the experience for those who do continue to use our site.

The only things at stake are an email address and your peace of mind. We strongly encourage you register to stay adequately protected against potential threats to the security of your personally identifiable information.
